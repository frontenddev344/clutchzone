Erling Haaland's Remarkable Form Continues with Another Scoring Streak

Erling Haaland is on fire, with his recent performances reinforcing his status as one of the top fantasy football assets. With City's attacking fixtures coming up, Haaland's current form suggests he will remain a key player for fantasy teams, making him a must-have for those looking to climb the rankings.
