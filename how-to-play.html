How to Play

Step-by-step: How to Play Clutch Zone

Step 1: Register

Create an account on the platform. You need this to start building your team and competing in leagues.

Step 2: Pick Your Team

You get a budget to assemble a team of real football players. Choose your starting 11 and substitutes carefully, and balance your budget with top performers and hidden gems.

Step 3: Understand the Points System

Players earn points based on their actual performances in matches. Goals, assists, clean sheets, and saves all contribute points. But be careful â€“ mistakes, cards, and missed penalties can lose points.

Step 4: Choose a Captain

Select a captain each gameweek. Your captain earns double points, so pick someone likely to perform well. You can also choose a vice-captain as a backup.

Step 5: Make Weekly Transfers

Each gameweek you can make transfers to swap out underperforming or injured players. Most leagues give you a limited number of free transfers, with additional changes costing points.

Step 6: Set Your Lineup

Before each gameweek deadline, finalize your starting 11 and make sure your team is ready. Check fixtures, player form, and injuries to set the best possible lineup.

Step 7: Join a League

You can join public or private leagues to compete with friends or other players. Some leagues are points-based, while others may have head-to-head matchups.

Step 8: Track Player Performance

After each match, your players' performances will be updated in the game. Check your team's score to see how your players did, and compare your progress in your league standings.

Step 9: Use Wildcards and Boosters

Throughout the season, you'll have Wildcards (unlimited transfers) and special chips like Bench Boost (all your players score points) or Triple Captain (your captain scores triple points). Use these strategically.

Step 10: Win and Rank

Compete every week, climb the league tables, and aim to finish as high as possible by the end of the season. Some leagues offer prizes, while others are just for bragging rights.
