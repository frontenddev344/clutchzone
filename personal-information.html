Personal Information Policy

At Clutch Zone, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Collection of Personal Information

We may collect personal information from you when you:

  • Register on our website

  • Subscribe to our newsletter

  • Contact us for support or inquiries

The types of information we may collect include:

  • Name

  • Email address

  • Contact details

Use of Personal Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To communicate with you regarding your account or your inquiries

  • To provide updates and information related to our services

  • To improve our website and user experience

Protection of Your Information

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Your data is stored securely and is only accessible to authorized personnel.

Sharing of Personal Information

We assure you that we do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data will only be shared under the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with trusted service providers who help us operate our website. These providers are obligated to keep your information safe and confidential.

  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights and the rights of others.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we have about you

  • Request corrections of any inaccuracies in your data

  • Withdraw your consent for us to use your information

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it regularly.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact us at info@ClutchZone.org.
